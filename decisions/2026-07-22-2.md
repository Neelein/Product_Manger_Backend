# Move ListMembers to ChatRoomRepository; remove memberRepo from ChatRoomHandler

## Summary
Moved `ListMembersNotInRoom` and `CountMembersNotInRoom` from `MemberRepository`/`member_repo.go` to `ChatRoomRepository`/`chat_repo.go`. `ChatRoomHandler` no longer depends on `memberRepo` — it calls `h.repo.ListMembersNotInRoom` instead.

## Changes
- `domain/repository.go`: moved `ListMembersNotInRoom` and `CountMembersNotInRoom` from `MemberRepository` to `ChatRoomRepository`
- `database/member_repo.go`: removed `memberRow`, `ListMembersNotInRoom`, `CountMembersNotInRoom`
- `database/chat_repo.go`: added `memberRow` struct, `ListMembersNotInRoom` and `CountMembersNotInRoom` on `ChatRoomRepositoryPGX`
- `api/chat_handler.go`: removed `memberRepo` field; simplified `NewChatRoomHandler(repo)`; calls `h.repo.ListMembersNotInRoom` / `h.repo.CountMembersNotInRoom`
- `api/router.go`: `NewChatRoomHandler(repo)` no longer needs `memberRepo`
- `test/api/chat_handler_test.go`: updated `setupChatTest` to not pass `memberRepo`

## Why
`ListMembersNotInRoom` is conceptually about chat rooms (listing members available to add to a room), not about member CRUD. Moving it to `ChatRoomRepository` removes an unnecessary dependency from `ChatRoomHandler`.
